In the course of managing advertising campaigns on behalf of Clients, we may access data from advertising platforms including Google Ads, Meta (Facebook/Instagram), and LinkedIn. This data is used solely to manage and optimize campaigns and is governed by those platforms' respective terms of service and privacy policies.

02How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

03Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity — processing required to perform a contract with you or take steps prior to entering one
  • Legitimate interests — processing necessary for our legitimate business interests, such as responding to inquiries and improving our services, where those interests are not overridden by your rights
  • Legal obligation — processing required to comply with applicable law
  • Consent — where we have obtained your consent for specific processing activities

04Information Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We may share information with trusted third-party vendors who assist us in operating our business (e.g., email platforms, payment processors, scheduling tools), subject to confidentiality obligations

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

05Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and analyze site traffic. Cookies are small data files stored on your device.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic site functionality
  • Analytics cookies: Used to understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Marketing cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertising and track campaign performance (e.g., Google Ads, Meta Pixel)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain site functionality. You may also opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We use Meta Pixel and Google tag tracking on this website to measure the effectiveness of our own marketing. If you have visited this site, your browser data may be used to show you relevant advertising on those platforms. You can manage your ad preferences via Meta's Ad Preferences and Google's Ad Settings.

06Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, maintain our business records, and comply with legal obligations. Specifically:

  • Client contact and project data is retained for a minimum of 5 years following project completion
  • Website analytics data is retained per the default retention settings of the applicable analytics platform
  • Marketing inquiry data is retained for up to 2 years if no engagement is established

Upon request, we will delete or anonymize your personal data unless retention is required by law.

07Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at jose@thealphacreative.com. We will respond within 30 days.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security and are not responsible for unauthorized access resulting from circumstances beyond our reasonable control.

09Artificial Intelligence & Data Use Policy

Our Commitment: AlphaCreative uses AI tools to deliver better, faster results for our clients. We will never sell, license, or share your data with third parties for AI training purposes, and we will never use your confidential business information outside the scope of your engagement.

How We Use AI in Our Work

AlphaCreative uses artificial intelligence tools and agents as part of our service delivery workflow. These tools help us research, analyze, strategize, and produce work more effectively on your behalf. AI-assisted work may include:

  • Market and competitor research and analysis
  • Draft creation for ad copy, messaging, and strategic documents
  • Data analysis and performance reporting
  • Campaign structure recommendations and optimization inputs
  • Internal workflow automation and process management

All AI-assisted deliverables are reviewed, validated, and approved by a qualified AlphaCreative team member before delivery to you. We do not deliver raw AI output as a final work product.

How We Train and Improve Our AI Workflows

AlphaCreative continuously refines the AI agents and prompting systems we use to better serve our clients. This improvement process may involve:

  • Training our internal AI workflows using anonymized and aggregated patterns, examples, and learnings from past engagements
  • Refining our systems using generalized insights about what strategies and approaches produce effective results
  • Updating our AI configurations based on industry trends and platform changes

When we use engagement learnings to improve our AI systems, we do so only in a manner that removes or anonymizes identifying information. Your specific business data, client names, financials, strategies, or proprietary information are never used in identifiable form to train or improve AI systems.

What We Will Never Do

  • Sell, license, rent, or transfer your data to any AI platform, data broker, or third party for training or commercial purposes
  • Use your confidential business information to benefit any other client or third party
  • Submit your proprietary strategies, financial data, or client lists into public or shared AI models in an identifiable way
  • Allow any AI platform to retain or use your data for purposes beyond the immediate task for which it was submitted, to the extent we are able to control this through platform settings and agreements

Third-Party AI Platforms

In delivering our services, we may use third-party AI tools and platforms (such as large language models, analytics AI, or automation platforms). These platforms are subject to their own terms of service and privacy policies. We take care to:

  • Use enterprise or privacy-configured versions of AI tools where available
  • Avoid inputting personally identifiable client information into third-party AI tools unnecessarily
  • Review the data practices of any AI tool we deploy in client work

Upon request, we will disclose which AI platforms were used in connection with your specific engagement.

Your Right to Opt Out

If you prefer that your engagement be conducted without the use of AI-assisted tools, you may request this in writing at any time. We will honor such requests to the extent operationally feasible and will communicate any impact on timelines or deliverables. Please contact us at jose@thealphacreative.com.
